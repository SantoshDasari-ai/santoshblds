Rx Bottle Opener

Project Overview

An innovative device designed to assist in opening and closing medical bottles, specifically targeting individuals with limited hand dexterity due to conditions such as arthritis, carpal tunnel syndrome, or age-related challenges.

Abstract

Many individuals who receive prescription medications in standard orange bottles face difficulties opening them—particularly those suffering from conditions like arthritis, old age-related issues, or carpal tunnel syndrome. Our device addresses this challenge by simplifying the opening process to a two-button operation. The mechanism utilizes motors to automatically handle the push-and-twist motion, eliminating the need for manual force.

Key Features

  • Two-button operation for easy opening and closing
  • Automated push-and-twist mechanism
  • Minimal force required from user
  • Compatible with standard prescription bottles
  • Motor-powered operation
